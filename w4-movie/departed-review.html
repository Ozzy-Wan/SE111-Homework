REVIEW

Dylan Osborn

April 2026

The Departed is one of my favorite crime movies because it keeps the pressure high the entire time. The story of an undercover cop and a mole inside the police department makes every scene feel tense, because both sides are constantly trying to figure each other out before they get exposed.

What really makes The Departed stand out to me is the cast and the atmosphere. The movie feels gritty, dangerous, and unpredictable, and the performances make the characters feel real. It is the kind of movie that stays intense all the way through and still holds up every time I watch it.

Trivia

  • The Departed won the Academy Award for Best Picture.
  • The movie is based on the Hong Kong crime film Infernal Affairs.
  • Martin Scorsese won his first Best Director Oscar for this film.
