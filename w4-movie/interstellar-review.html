REVIEW

Dylan Osborn

April 2026

Interstellar is one of my favorite movies because it combines massive scientific ideas with a very personal story. The film does a great job showing how small humans are compared to the universe while still focusing on relationships and sacrifice.

What really makes Interstellar stand out to me is the way it balances realism with emotion. The visuals, especially scenes like the black hole, feel incredible, but the story between Cooper and his daughter is what makes the movie unforgettable.

Trivia

  • The black hole in Interstellar was created using real scientific equations.
  • Time moves slower near the black hole — one hour on Miller’s planet equals seven years on Earth.
  • Many of the space scenes were designed to be as scientifically accurate as possible.
