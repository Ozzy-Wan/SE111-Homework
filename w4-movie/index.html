Interstellar

Interstellar is a science fiction film about a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity as Earth becomes less habitable. The movie mixes survival, family, time, and exploration in a way that makes it emotional as well as visually impressive.

Company Credits: Paramount Pictures, Warner Bros.

Release Date: November 7, 2014

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Sci-Fi

Rating: PG-13

Running Time: 169 min

The Departed

The Departed is a crime thriller about an undercover cop and a mole within the police department, both trying to uncover the other before they are exposed. The movie is intense, suspenseful, and full of strong performances and tension.

Company Credits: Warner Bros., Plan B Entertainment

Release Date: October 6, 2006

Genres: Crime, Drama, Thriller

Rating: R

Running Time: 151 min
